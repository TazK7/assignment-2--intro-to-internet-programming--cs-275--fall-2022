Over The Counter

Sleep drugs you can buy without a prescription aren't much safer than Rx drugs. In fact, it's easy to take high doses of them because their active ingrediants-- the antihistamines diphenhydramine and doxylamine-- aren't the only in OTC sleep products such as Sominex and ZzzQuil. They're also in nighttime pain relievers such as Advil PM and Tylenom PM, as well as allergy meds such as Benadryl.
